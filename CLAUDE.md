## Design Context

### Users

Arabic-speaking online shoppers across the Gulf and MENA region. They are deal-seekers — motivated by savings, driven by discovery. They arrive with intent: "I want to buy something and I want the best price." Context is mobile-first, Arabic-native, and time-sensitive. Job to be done: **find a valid coupon fast, feel the win, move on to checkout**.

### Brand Personality

**Bold. Generous. Alive.**

Couponawy should feel like the friend who always knows the deal — enthusiastic, dependable, never boring. Emotional goal: a small burst of delight on every visit — the feeling of getting away with something good.

Anti-personality: clinical, corporate, sleepy, overly minimal, purple-tech-startup.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Vibrant + Editorial Arabic** — Groupon/RetailMeNot energy, designed for Arabic reading patterns and cultural context. Light mode only.

Fresh color system (the existing violet palette and misleading token names are wrong — replace entirely):
- **Primary**: Bold crimson-coral `oklch(52% 0.23 22)` — energy, urgency, deals
- **Accent**: Warm amber/gold `oklch(72% 0.17 75)` — treasure, value, celebration
- **Surface**: Warm white (not lavender, not pure white)
- **Text**: Deep charcoal (not navy, not pure black)

### Design Principles

1. **The deal is always the hero.** Discount %, coupon codes, and savings amounts get maximum visual weight.
2. **Urgency without pressure.** Bold hierarchy creates momentum — not anxiety. No fake timers. No red everywhere.
3. **Arabic-first.** RTL is a first-class citizen. Typography breathes. Noto Kufi Arabic (display), Tajawal (body).
4. **Scan in 3 seconds.** Users identify the best coupon on any page in under 3 seconds.
5. **Trust through clarity.** Clean surfaces, visible store branding, no dark patterns. Reliability is a competitive advantage.
